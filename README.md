# SuperStore Sales Performance Dashboard
## Task 1 - Business Sales Performance Analytics

### 📌 Objective
Analyze business sales data to identify revenue trends, 
top-selling products, high-value categories, and regional 
performance.

### 🛠️ Tools Used
- Google Sheets (Data cleaning & Pivot Tables)
- Power BI (Dashboard & Visualizations)

### 📊 Key Findings
- Technology leads sales at 37.5% of total revenue
- Central region is the top performing region at $2.8M
- Apple Smart Phone is the best selling product at $86,936
- Sales grew 90% from 2011 to 2014

### 💡 Recommendations
- Invest more in Technology category
- Focus marketing efforts on Central region
- Expand Smart Phone product line
- Maintain growth momentum going into 2015
